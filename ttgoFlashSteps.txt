Requirements for flashing TTGO Tbeams V1.1s
------------------------------------------------

Place relevant libararies (from LoRaDrifers GitHub) into Arduino/Libraries

Follow steps here:
https://docs.espressif.com/projects/arduino-esp32/en/latest/installing.html#installing-using-boards-manager

AXP202 library:
https://github.com/lyusupov/SoftRF/tree/master/software/firmware/source/libraries/AXP202X_Library

Setting up symlinks
Windows:
run symlinks.bat from LoRa_drifters directory. This creates symlinks for the libraries shared between master and servant
Mac/linux (try this from terminal): 
ln -s loraDrifterMaster/src/ src/
ln -s loraDrifterServant/src/ src/
